About RugProof

RugProof (RUGP) is a BNB Smart Chain token built to combat fraud in crypto. By empowering the community with tools, education, and incentives, RugProof stands as a first line of defense against scams and rug pulls. It promotes safe investing practices while providing the foundation for a decentralized scam reporting and tracking system.
